Old Fashioned

A timeless classic known for its simplicity and sophistication.

Why Drink It?

The Old Fashioned is a classic cocktail that highlights the rich flavors of bourbon or rye whiskey. Its straightforward ingredients make it a sophisticated choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange twist for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Muddle the sugar cube and bitters in a glass.
  2. Add whiskey and stir.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and stir until chilled.
  4. Garnish with an orange twist.

Tips for the Perfect Old Fashioned

  • Use a high-quality whiskey for the best flavor.
  • Muddle the sugar and bitters well to ensure the flavors blend.
  • Stir the drink until well chilled for a smoother taste.
