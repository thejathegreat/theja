Honey Strawberry Lemonade

A sweet and tangy lemonade with a burst of strawberry flavor!

Why Drink It?

Strawberry lemonade is a refreshing and naturally sweetened drink, perfect for summer days. The honey adds a touch of natural sweetness while the strawberries and lemon create a delightful balance of tart and sweet.

Health Benefits

Strawberries are packed with antioxidants and Vitamin C, while honey provides natural sweetness and potential immune-boosting properties. Lemons also offer plenty of Vitamin C to help keep you healthy.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz fresh strawberry puree
  • 3 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Water or sparkling water
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. In a pitcher, mix strawberry puree, lemon juice, and honey.
  2. Add water or sparkling water and stir well.
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh strawberries.

Tips for the Perfect Strawberry Lemonade

  • Use fresh, ripe strawberries for the best flavor.
  • For a lighter drink, use sparkling water instead of still water.
  • Adjust the sweetness by adding more or less honey to your taste.
