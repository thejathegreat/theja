Long Island Iced Tea

A potent and flavorful cocktail that combines multiple spirits with a splash of cola.

Why Drink It?

The Long Island Iced Tea is famous for its strong kick and delicious blend of spirits. Itâ€™s a great choice for those who enjoy a bold cocktail with a unique mix of flavors.

Health Benefits

Due to its high alcohol content, it's best enjoyed in moderation. The drink does not offer significant health benefits but can be a fun and flavorful option for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 oz vodka
  • 0.5 oz rum
  • 0.5 oz gin
  • 0.5 oz tequila
  • 0.5 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • Cola
  • Ice
  • Lemon wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with cola and garnish with a lemon wedge.

Tips for the Perfect Long Island Iced Tea

  • Use fresh lemon juice for a better flavor.
  • Adjust the amount of cola to your taste preference.
  • Ensure all spirits are chilled for a more refreshing drink.
