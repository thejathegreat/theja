Pomegranate Mulled Cider

A warm, spiced drink perfect for cozying up during the colder months.

Why Drink It?

This mulled cider blends the tangy sweetness of pomegranate with the warmth of spices like cinnamon and cloves. It's a wonderful, non-alcoholic alternative for the winter months and holiday gatherings.

Health Benefits

Pomegranate juice offers a wealth of antioxidants and Vitamin C, while the spices in the cider can have warming, anti-inflammatory properties.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz apple cider
  • 4 oz pomegranate juice
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • Orange slice for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small pot, combine apple cider, pomegranate juice, cinnamon stick, and cloves.
  2. Heat on low until warm and aromatic (do not boil).
  3. Pour into a mug and garnish with an orange slice.

Tips for the Perfect Pomegranate Mulled Cider

  • Simmer the cider on low to prevent it from becoming too bitter.
  • Add a dash of maple syrup for extra sweetness if desired.
  • Strain the spices before serving for a smoother drink.
