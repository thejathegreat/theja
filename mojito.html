Mojito

A refreshing minty cocktail, perfect for summer!

Why Drink It?

The Mojito is a timeless classic known for its refreshing qualities. The combination of mint, lime, and rum creates a rejuvenating drink that's ideal for hot weather. Its crisp flavor makes it a popular choice for summer parties and casual gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Mint leaves
  • Club soda
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice.
  2. Add rum and fill the glass with ice.
  3. Top with club soda and garnish with mint leaves.

Tips for the Perfect Mojito

  • Use fresh mint for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the sugar to your taste preference.
  • Chill the glass before serving for a more refreshing experience.
