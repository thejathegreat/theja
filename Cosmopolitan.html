Cosmopolitan

A stylish and tart cocktail that's perfect for a night out.

Why Drink It?

The Cosmopolitan is a chic cocktail known for its vibrant color and balanced flavors. It's ideal for social occasions and adds a touch of sophistication to any gathering.

Health Benefits

Though primarily a cocktail, the Cosmopolitan's cranberry juice provides antioxidants. Consume responsibly to enjoy the flavor without overindulging in alcohol.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • 0.5 oz triple sec
  • 0.5 oz lime juice
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Shake vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and lime juice with ice.
  2. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime twist or cranberry.

Tips for the Perfect Cosmopolitan

  • Use fresh lime juice for a tangy flavor.
  • Chill your glass in advance for a crisp presentation.
  • Adjust cranberry juice for desired sweetness.
