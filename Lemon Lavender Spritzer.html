Lemon Lavender Spritzer

A calming and fragrant spritzer, perfect for a relaxing day!

Why Drink It?

The Lemon Lavender Spritzer is a soothing and refreshing beverage, combining the zest of lemon with the calming aroma of lavender. Itâ€™s perfect for a sunny afternoon or a quiet moment to yourself.

Health Benefits

Lemon juice is packed with Vitamin C, which helps boost immunity. Lavender is known for its relaxing properties, which can help reduce stress and improve sleep quality. Together, they make a delightful, calming drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lavender syrup
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Lemon slice for garnish
  • Fresh lavender for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a glass, combine lemon juice and lavender syrup with ice.
  2. Top with club soda and stir gently.
  3. Garnish with a lemon slice and fresh lavender if desired.

Tips for the Perfect Lemon Lavender Spritzer

  • Adjust the lavender syrup to your preferred sweetness.
  • Use sparkling water for a lighter, less sweet option.
  • For a stronger lavender flavor, steep dried lavender in hot water and let it cool before adding to the spritzer.
