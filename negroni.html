Negroni

A classic Italian cocktail with a perfect balance of bitterness and sweetness.

Why Drink It?

The Negroni is renowned for its sophisticated flavor profile, blending bitter, sweet, and herbal notes. Itâ€™s a great choice for those who enjoy a strong, flavorful cocktail.

Health Benefits

While a Negroni is a cocktail with a strong taste, its main ingredients, like gin, vermouth, and Campari, have unique botanical properties. However, consume responsibly due to its alcohol content.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • Orange twist
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Stir gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth with ice in a mixing glass.
  2. Strain into a chilled glass over ice.
  3. Garnish with an orange twist.

Tips for the Perfect Negroni

  • Use high-quality gin for a better flavor.
  • Adjust the Campari and vermouth to balance bitterness and sweetness.
  • Garnish with a fresh orange twist for a citrusy aroma.
