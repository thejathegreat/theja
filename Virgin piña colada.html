Virgin Piña Colada

A creamy, tropical drink that's alcohol-free and oh-so-refreshing!

Why Drink It?

This tropical delight offers the classic blend of coconut and pineapple, but without the alcohol. It’s perfect for anyone who wants to enjoy the rich, creamy flavors of a Piña Colada in a non-alcoholic version.

Health Benefits

Made with fresh pineapple juice and coconut milk, this drink provides a good source of vitamins and healthy fats. Pineapple is rich in Vitamin C, while coconut milk offers healthy fats and electrolytes.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 oz coconut milk
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice
  • Pineapple slice for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine pineapple juice, coconut milk, and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass and garnish with a pineapple slice.

Tips for the Perfect Virgin Piña Colada

  • Use full-fat coconut milk for a richer texture.
  • Add frozen pineapple chunks for a thicker, more slushy drink.
  • Sweeten to taste with honey or a sugar substitute.
