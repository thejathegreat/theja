Classic Shirley Temple

A fun, fruity, and fizzy non-alcoholic drink that's perfect for all ages!

Why Drink It?

The Shirley Temple is a nostalgic favorite known for its sweet, citrus flavor and playful appearance. Itâ€™s perfect for kids and adults alike and can be enjoyed at parties or as a refreshing treat any time.

Health Benefits

Though sweet, this drink can be made healthier by reducing the amount of grenadine and adding more real fruit juice. It also provides a quick energy boost due to its sugar content.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz ginger ale or lemon-lime soda
  • 1 oz grenadine
  • Maraschino cherries for garnish
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice and pour in ginger ale or lemon-lime soda.
  2. Add grenadine and stir gently.
  3. Garnish with maraschino cherries.

Tips for the Perfect Shirley Temple

  • For a healthier version, use natural cherry juice instead of grenadine.
  • Add a splash of orange juice for extra citrus flavor.
  • Chill the soda beforehand to keep the drink fizzy longer.
