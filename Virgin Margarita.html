Virgin Margarita

A zesty, refreshing margarita without the alcohol!

Why Drink It?

The Virgin Margarita offers all the refreshing tang of a classic margarita without the alcohol. It’s perfect for those who want a fun and refreshing drink at parties or for a relaxing afternoon.

Health Benefits

With lime juice being rich in Vitamin C and no alcohol involved, it’s a great immunity booster. Plus, it’s low in calories, making it a guilt-free indulgence!

Ingredients

  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz orange juice
  • 1 tsp agave syrup
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a shaker, combine lime juice, orange juice, and agave syrup with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with club soda and garnish with a lime wedge.

Tips for the Perfect Virgin Margarita

  • Use fresh lime juice for a tangier taste.
  • Adjust the sweetness by adding more or less agave syrup to suit your taste.
  • For an extra touch, rim the glass with salt before serving.
