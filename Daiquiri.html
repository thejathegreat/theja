Daiquiri

A classic cocktail that is both tangy and sweet, perfect for any occasion.

Why Drink It?

The Daiquiri is a timeless cocktail that offers a balanced mix of sweetness and acidity. Its simplicity makes it a versatile choice for various settings, from casual to elegant events.

Health Benefits

While the Daiquiri is enjoyed for its taste rather than health benefits, using fresh lime juice adds a dose of Vitamin C to the drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup
  • Ice
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine rum, lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Tips for the Perfect Daiquiri

  • Use fresh lime juice for the best flavor.
  • Shake the cocktail vigorously to ensure a well-mixed drink.
  • Adjust the simple syrup based on your sweetness preference.
